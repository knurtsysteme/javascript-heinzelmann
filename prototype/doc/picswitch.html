Literal: Picswitch

Download Picswitch.js
since: 0.20080626
requires: prototype
author: Daniel Oltmanns
version: 0.20080823
copyright: KNURT Systeme
license: GPL 3
Realize a rollover image with all images. The convention is, that the name of the rollover pic is the name of the normal pic followed by ("_over"). If you have a pic "logo.gif", it what be "logo_over.gif" then.
Our example shows the Logo of KNURT Systeme as an Rollover (Watch):
1<html>
2    <head>
3        <title>Picswitch Demo</title>
4        <script type="text/javascript" src="pathto/prototype.js"></script>
5        <script type="text/javascript" src="pathto/jsscrollwork/Picswitch.js"></script>
6        <script type="text/javascript">
7            window.onload = function() {
8                Picswitch.init();
9            }
10        </script>
11    </head>
12    <body>
13        <img src="KNURT_Systeme.gif" class="picswitch" />
14    </body>
15</html>

function: init(classname)

Initialize the rollover effects on the page.
param string classname:
   the value of the class attribute of the ImageElement, that shall be a rollover. This is optional (Default name is "picswitch").
